Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic and simple pasta dish loved around the world. It is made with long pasta noodles served with a rich tomato sauce and flavorful seasonings. This recipe is easy to prepare and perfect for a quick homemade meal with a traditional Italian-inspired taste.

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
  2. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, then simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and mix it with the sauce
  6. Serve with parmesan cheese and fresh basil on top.
