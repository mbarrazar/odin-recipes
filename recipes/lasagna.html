Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, tomato sauce, and melted cheese. It is a hearty and delicious meal that is perfect for sharing with family and friends. This version combines seasoned ground beef with mozzarella and ricotta cheese for a creamy texture and traditional flavor.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauces
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat to oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the onion and garlic in a pan with olive oil.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, salt and pepper, then cook for a few minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, add a layer of pasta, follow by meat, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
  8. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
