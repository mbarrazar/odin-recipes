Meatballs

Description

Meatballs are a flavorful and comforting dish made with seasoned ground meat, breadcrumbs, and spices. They are commonly served with pasta and tomato sauce, making them a classic homemade meal. This recipe creates juicy meatballs with a rich flavor that pairs perfectly with spaghetti or garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground beef, egg, breadcrumbs, garlic, parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the mixture into small meatballs.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the meatballs until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the tomato sauce to the pan.
  5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the meatballs are fully cooked.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
  7. Serve with pasta, rice, or bread.
